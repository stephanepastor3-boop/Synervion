# Synervion LinkedIn Quality Guidelines

## Voice & Tone
- **Professional & Scientific**: We are a research-driven biotechnology brand.
- **Accessible**: Complex science must be explained simply, but not dumbed down.
- **Authentic**: Avoid "AI-sounding" fluff like "Unlock the power of...", "In today's world...", or "Game-changer".
- **No Hard Sell**: We educate first. The product is the solution, not the pitch.

## Formatting Rules
- **Hook**: First line must be a thumb-stopper. A surprising fact, a bold statement, or a question.
- **Structure**:
  - Short paragraphs (1-2 sentences max).
  - Use whitespace generously.
  - Use bullet points for easy scanning.
- **Hashtags**: Use 3-5 relevant hashtags at the bottom. Do not clutter the text with them.
- **Call to Action (CTA)**: Soft CTA. "What's your experience with...?" or "Learn more at synervion.com".
