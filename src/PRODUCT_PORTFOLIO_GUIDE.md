## üé® Visual Styling Guidelines

### Product Photography Standards

**Background:**
- Clean white or soft neutral gray
- Gradient backgrounds (white to light gray)
- Never busy or distracting

**Lighting:**
- Soft, diffused natural light
- Highlight product labels and badges
- Subtle shadows for depth

**Composition:**
- Center-aligned or slightly off-center
- Group shots show product variety
- Individual shots emphasize quality

**Post-Processing:**
- Minimal editing (authentic representation)
- Color correction to match brand palette
- Clean cutouts for web use
